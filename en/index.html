ProteusOS v1.0 - Aegis

ProteusOS is a Debian 12 (XFCE) based distribution designed for rapid Security Operations Center (SOC) deployment. A hardened, minimal environment ready for analysts.

⬇ Download ISO (v1.0) GitHub ↗

Installation Profiles

LITE

SMBs & Basic Perimeter protection.

  • Wazuh (SIEM/XDR)
  • CrowdSec (Modern IPS)
  • AdGuard Home
  • Tailscale

STANDARD

Automation & Network Inspection.

  • Everything in LITE +
  • Suricata (IDS/IPS)
  • Shuffle (SOAR)

FULL

Total Forensics & Intelligence.

  • Everything in STANDARD +
  • Zeek (Network Mon)
  • TheHive & Cortex

Hardware Requirements

Minimum (LITE)

  • 2 vCPU / 4 GB RAM
  • 40 GB SSD

Optimal (FULL)

  • 4+ vCPU / 16 GB RAM
  • 100 GB+ SSD
