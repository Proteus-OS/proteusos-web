ProteusOS v1.0 - Aegis

Deploy your own SOC without friction. Free, structural, and accessible to everyone.

ProteusOS is a distribution based on Debian 13 (XFCE), optimized for cybersecurity analysts. It provides a hardened ecosystem for threat detection and incident response without compromising performance.

Why ProteusOS?

  • ✅ For real environments: Ideal for deployments with low budgets or limited resources.
  • ✅ Learning curve: Perfect for learning how a SOC works by centralizing tools, without dealing with complex manual configurations.
  • ✅ Dynamic Deployment: Adaptable profiles (Lite, Standard, Full) based on your infrastructure needs.

Deployment Profiles

ProteusOS LITE

Endpoint and perimeter protection. Optimized native installation.

  • Wazuh: Unified SIEM/XDR.
  • CrowdSec: Collaborative IPS.
  • AdGuard Home: DNS Sinkhole.
  • Network Tools (Nmap/Tcpdump).

ProteusOS STANDARD

Network traffic inspection and security process automation.

  • Everything in LITE +
  • Suricata: Network IDS/IPS.
  • Shuffle: SOAR Automation.

ProteusOS FULL

Total forensic analysis and centralized threat intelligence.

  • Everything in STANDARD +
  • Zeek: Protocol Analysis.
  • TheHive & Cortex: Incident Management.

Hybrid Structural Architecture

ProteusOS maximizes efficiency through a dual deployment matrix. This is how the system layers are structured:

Container Layer (Docker)
Isolated environments for: Shuffle (SOAR) | TheHive & Cortex | AdGuard Home
Native Layer (Bare Metal / Systemd)
Zero latency for: Wazuh | Suricata | Zeek | CrowdSec | Tailscale
Base Operating System
Debian 13 + XFCE

Hardware Requirements
